Short Bio

Yue Cao is currently a researcher in Microsoft Research Asia, headed by Jifeng Dai. Prior to that, he received both B.S. degree and Ph.D. degree from the School of Software, Tsinghua University with honors, under supervision of Prof. Jianmin Wang and Prof. Mingsheng Long in 2014 and 2019.
