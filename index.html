About me

Yue Cao is currently a PhD candidate in Institute of Information System and Engineering, School of Software, Tsinghua University, under supervision of Prof. Jianmin Wang and Doctor Mingsheng Long. He received his bachelor degree of Science majoring in Computer Software in Tsinghua University in July, 2014. His research interests are Deep Learning, Transfer Learning and Large Scale Similarity Search.
